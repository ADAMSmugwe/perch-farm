Quality Poultry Farming Since 2010

Founded in 2010, Perch Poultry Farm has grown from a small family operation to one of Eldoret's most trusted poultry producers. Our farm spans over 50 acres of lush Kenyan countryside, providing the perfect environment for our chickens to thrive.

We believe in sustainable farming practices that prioritize animal welfare, environmental responsibility, and community development. All our poultry is raised without antibiotics or hormones, with access to open spaces and natural feed.

Our Story

Learn more about our journey, values, and commitment to excellence in poultry farming.

From Humble Beginnings

Perch Poultry Farm was founded in 2010 by Bob and Gladys Toroitich with just 50 chickens in their backyard. What began as a small family project to provide eggs for their local community has grown into one of Eldoret's most respected poultry operations.

Today, we manage over 10,000 chickens across our 50-acre farm, supplying fresh eggs and poultry to households, restaurants, and supermarkets throughout Uasin Gishu County and beyond.

Despite our growth, we've maintained our commitment to small-scale, ethical farming practices that prioritize animal welfare and environmental sustainability.

Our Values

The principles that guide everything we do at Perch Poultry Farm.

Animal Welfare

We provide spacious, clean environments with access to outdoor areas. Our chickens are never caged and are free to express natural behaviors.

Sustainability

We use solar power, rainwater harvesting, and compost all waste to minimize our environmental impact and create a closed-loop system.

Community

We support local farmers through training programs and source all our feed from nearby suppliers to strengthen the regional economy.
